PANI PURI

Ingredients: Puri shells, Tamarind pulp, Mint leaves, Cumin powder, Potato, Chickpeas, Salt, Sugar, Water.

Instructions: Prepare the filling by mashing potatoes and chickpeas with spices. Fill the puris with the mixture, top with tamarind water, and mint water. Serve immediately.

MOMOS

Ingredients: Flour, Cabbage, Carrot, Onion, Ginger, Garlic, Soy Sauce, Salt, Pepper.

Instructions: Prepare the filling with grated vegetables, garlic, and soy sauce. Stuff the mixture into the dough wrappers and steam. Serve with a spicy dipping sauce.
